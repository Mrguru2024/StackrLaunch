Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Stackr

How secure is Stackr?

Stackr implements bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data, both in transit and at rest. We use secure, read-only connections to financial institutions and never store your banking credentials. Our platform undergoes regular security audits and complies with SOC 2 standards.

How much time will Stackr save me?

Most users report saving 10-15 hours per month on financial tasks. This includes time previously spent on manual expense tracking, invoice management, receipt organization, and basic bookkeeping. The exact time savings depend on your business volume and complexity.

What financial accounts can I connect?

Stackr connects with over 12,000 financial institutions, including major banks, credit unions, payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Square), credit cards, and investment accounts. If you use it for your business finances, we likely support it.

Does Stackr replace my accountant?

Stackr works alongside your accountant, not as a replacement. We handle the day-to-day financial organization and provide clean, categorized data that makes your accountant's job easier. Many accountants actually recommend Stackr to their clients to improve data quality and reduce their billable hours.

When will Stackr be available?

Stackr is currently in closed beta with a limited number of users. We expect to launch publicly in Q3 2023. By joining our waitlist now, you'll secure early access and founding member benefits, including lifetime discounted pricing and premium features.
